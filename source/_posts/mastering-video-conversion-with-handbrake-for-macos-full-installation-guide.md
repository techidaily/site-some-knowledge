## Download and Install HandBrake on Mac

Before downloading HandBrake on your Mac, please ensure that your Mac meets the minimum requirements as listed below:

Processor:

* AMD Ryzen, Threadripper, or Epyc
* Intel Core (6th generation and newer) i3, i5, i7, i9, or equivalent Xeon
* Apple Silicon M1 and newer, recent Intel Macs supported per the above

Free memory:

* Depends on settings used but as a general guide:
* 1 GB for transcoding standard definition video (480p/576p)
* 2 to 8 GB for transcoding high definition video (720p/1080p)
* 6 to 16 GB or more for transcoding ultra high definition video (2160p 4K)

System storage:

* 150 MB for the HandBrake app
* 10 GB or more recommended for processing and storing your new videos

Display resolution:

* 1280x960 minimum, proportionately higher with display scaling.

## How to Use HandBrake on Mac

Once you have successfully installed HandBrake on your Mac, you can utilize it to convert videos, DVDs, or Blu-rays. Here is a step-by-step guide:

**Step 1.** Launch HandBrake.

**Step 2.** Click the Open Source button on the toolbar to browse and open the video, DVD, or Blu-ray you want to convert.

**Step 3.** Use HandBrake's Title control to select the title you want to convert. DVD and Blu-ray discs often have more than one titles.

**Step 4.** Click the drop-down menu of Preset to choose a preset based on your own needs.

**Step 5.** Click the Browse… button to specify the location for the resulting file.

**Step 6.** Click the Start button on the toolbar to begin converting your media.
