### Part 1\. Convert DRM-Free EPUB to PDF with Calibre

1[Download Calibre](http://calibre-ebook.com/download) and install it on your computer.

![convert epub to pdf calibre](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/convert-epub-to-pdf-calibre.jpg)

2 Run Calibre and add the books to it.

Open Calibre, click the "Add books" button at the top left corner to add epub books that need to be converted to pdf. When you succeed in adding books, the title of the ebooks will be displayed in the main interface.

3 Select your epub books that you want to convert to pdf and then click the "Convert books" button.

![calibre steps](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/calibre-steps.jpg)

Choose PDF as the output format in the pop-up window and click the "OK" button.

![select-pdf](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/select-pdf.jpg)

Then the pop-up window will close and convert your book in the backstage. Look at the right bottom corner, you will see the message bar "Job 1", it means a conversion job is going on. As soon as the number becomes "0", the conversion is completed. 

![converting job](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/converting-job.jpg)

After it converts epub to pdf, you can see there are epub and pdf formats available in Formats category. You can click either epub or pdf for reading. If you don't know where the converted pdf files are saved, you can click the "Click to open" icon to find where they are.

![file path](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/file-path.jpg)

![successfully convert epub to pdf](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/successfully-convert-epub-to-pdf-calibre.jpg)

![drmed book](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/drmed-book.jpg)

**Step 3.** Add the DeDRM plugins to Calibre.

Run Calibre, navigate to "Preferences" on the top of Calibre's interface.

#### Step 4\. Remove epub drm.

Now restart Calibre and add your DRMed books to it. Usually it will remove the books' DRM automatically. If your DRMed books are already in your Calibre library, you should remove them from Calibre first and then re-add them to Calibre, or their DRM will not be removed.

**Note:** Not all adobe drm can be removed with Calibre, for example, right now Calibre can no longer remove Nook DRM any more. so don't be panic if you fail to decrypt epub files, after all, it's a freeware. By the way, if you want to decrypt Kindle books with Calibre, you can read this step by step guide about [how to remove KFX DRM with Calibre](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/); and if you want to decrypt Kobo/Google Play books, you can read this guide about [how to remove DRM from Kobo/Google Play Book](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/).
