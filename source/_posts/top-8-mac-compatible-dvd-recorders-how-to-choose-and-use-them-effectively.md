Supposing you have a built-in DVD drive on Mac or an external [SuperDrive for Mac](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/), you can directly use Finder to burn files to DVD or CD for sharing or storage. 

* **Step 1:** Insert a blank disc into the [Mac DVD drive](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/).
* **Step 2:** Choose "Open Finder" on the pop-up menu and click "OK".
* **Step 3:** Double-click the blank disc to open its window; then drag the media files to the window.
* **Step 4:** Enter a name in the "Disc Name" field and tap "Burn". Your Mac is burning disc now.
