## To Make Photo Slideshow on Mac with Music, Follow the Instructions

**Step 1: Import the photos folder.**

 Firstly, you should put all the photos you want in a folder. It's best to arrange the photos in the order you want them to appear before you go into slideshow mode. Then click "add photos" button and select the entire folder for making the slideshow.

**Step 2: Select output format.**

 Select an output video format or gadget for your slideshow - AVI, MP4, MPEG, MKV, WMV, iPad, Android and more. Use the "Browse" button to specify destination folder for saving output files.
