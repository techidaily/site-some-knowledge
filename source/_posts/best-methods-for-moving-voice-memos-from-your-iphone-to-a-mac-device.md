## Send Voice Memos from iPhone to Mac with Share Button

**1.** Open Voice Memo app in your iPhone.   
**2.** Select the track you want to get off.  
**3\.** Tap the Share button and then Mail. Send voice memo to your email address.  
**4.** Open email on Mac and download the voice memo from iPhone. 

![sync iPhone voice memos with Share button](https://www.macxdvd.com/mobile/article-image/transfer-voice-memos-from-iphone-to-mac-1.jpg)

_**Tips:** It's a straight-forward way to export voice memos from iPhone to Mac without iTunes Syncing using email. But it's only proper for sending iPhone voice memos of **small size**. If your Mac supports Airdrop, you can export iPhone voice memos to Mac via Airdrop, which requires a Mac with OS X Yosemite or later and iOS 7 or later._ 

## How to Copy iPhone Voice Memos to Mac with Dropbox

 Apple's AirDrop makes it easy to transfer files between iDevices and Mac, without the need for third party tool. To extract voice memos from iPhone to Mac,

**1.** Open Voice Memo app on iPhone.   
**2.** Select the voice memo you want to move to Mac.   
**3.** Tap the Share button. Select "Save to Dropbox".   
**4.** Go to Dropbox on Mac and copy iPhone voice memos to Mac. 

![use Dropbox to sync voice memos](https://www.macxdvd.com/mobile/article-image/transfer-voice-memos-from-iphone-to-mac-2.jpg)

_Before you use Dropbox to copy voice memos from iPhone to Mac without iTunes, you'd better check out how much free space left. **Dropbox will stop syncing** iPhone voice memos to Mac when you are over your storage quota. You can also use Dropbox to [backup iPhone files](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/)._

## How to Extract Voice Memos from iPhone to Mac Using iTunes

**1.** Open iTunes, make sure you've upgraded to the latest version.  
**2.** Connect iPhone to Mac with included USB cable. You'll see your iPhone icon at the left corner after it is recognized by iTunes.  
**3.** Click iPhone icon and then Music under Settings.   
**4.** Check the box "Include voice memos".   
**5.** Click "Apply" and all the voice memos on iPhone will be exported to Mac.
