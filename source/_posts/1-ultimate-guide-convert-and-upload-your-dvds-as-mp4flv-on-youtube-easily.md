## Best Video Uploading Settings for YouTube 

Before you convert your DVD files to YouTube for uploading, you need to make sense of the supported YouTube file formats. Here's the list of some well-known [YouTube video formats](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) for uploading:

* **WebM** (VP8 video codec and Vorbis Audio codec)
* **MP4, 3GPP and MOV** files - (typically supporting h264 and mpeg4 video codecs and AAC audio codec)
* **AVI** (Many cameras output this format - typically the video codec is MJPEG and audio is PCM)
* **MPEGPS** (Typically supporting MPEG2 video codec and MP2 audio)
* **WMV** (Windows Media Video)
* **FLV** (Adobe - FLV1 video codec, MP3 audio)
* **DNxHR** (A lossy UHDTV post-production codec.)
* **CineForm** (An open source video codec developped for CineForm Inc.)
* **MPEGPS** (A container format for multiplexing digital audio, video and more.)
* **ProRes** (A high quality video compression format developed by Apple Inc.)
* **HEVC** (A video compression standard that halves the file size at the same level of video quality compared with H.264 AVC.)

Obviously, in order to upload the content to YouTube, you need 3rd party DVD to YouTube converter software to digitize DVD to a format that is supported by YouTube. Besides, you'd better have a good understanding of [YouTube video size](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) or [video setting](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) for uploading as below:

 \- Each video you are uploading should be **less than 15 minutes by default**. Google Account needs to be verified if you want to upload longer DVD to YouTube.  
 \- YouTube isn't too harsh on the video size and length. Even if your DVD video larger than 20GB can be uploaded to YouTube as long as you have the latest browser. The maximum file you can upload is **128GB or 12 hours**.

## How to Upload DVD Video to YouTube

After you get ready for your DVD video, go ahead to upload it to YouTube.

1. Sign up for a free YouTube membership, or login into YouTube if you've already had an account.
2. Click the record-shaped icon and tap "Upload Videos" at the upper right corner of YouTube page.
3. Choose the converted DVD video you want to upload to YouTube.
4. Enter basic information about your DVD video, including Title, Description, Tags, and Category. You can define your privacy setting and advanced setting before uploading the DVD video.
5. Click Publish button to begin the uploading. the speed depends on the file size, video resolution, bandwidth, and YouTube itself.
