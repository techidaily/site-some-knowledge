### I. Handbrake Won't Rip DVD

Majority of you might be bothered by this common issue: handbrake failed to rip DVD disc. Why would it happen? It mainly resulted from:

 1\. DVD disc is physically damaged or scratched. Or it's ruined with dust and mould.

2\. It's strongly protected by the latest DVD encryption tech. Handbrake is powerless to remove them though it has VLC or libdvdcss.

3\. There is something wrong with your handbrake settings (OpenCL resizer) or version (possibly malware or virus). 

4\. Handbrake you're using isn't well compatible with your operating system, especially for the latest macOS Big Sur/high sierra.
