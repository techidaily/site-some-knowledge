As a Kindle lover, like me, we are so familiar with these two formats, prc and mobi, yes? They are 2 of the kindle eBook formats, widely used and supported on kindle devices and apps.

But today my concern is not about the difference between them, but the conversion from prc to mobi. For why, and how to, follow this guide below please.

### Why we convert prc to mobi

Firstly both prc and mobi are ebook file extension created by Mobipocket, but since Amazon acquired them in 2005, these 2 formats are used more widely in kindle ebooks.

#### When will we get prc files?

If you downloaded your purchased kindle books on **Kindle Fire or Android devices** **with Kindle for Android app**, your downloaded files would be PRC format.

PRC file is just a "container" format, a little like a "zip" file. And among our downloaded prc books, some of them may be drm free, and the other may be DRM protected. DRM free prc are nearly the same as mobi, but DRM protected prc are different.

#### When will we get mobi files?

Mobi format was firstly developed for the Mobipocket Reader, but now it is used by Amazon with a little different DRM scheme and called AZW. In other words, when DRM free, mobi is mobi; when DRM added, books will be mobi, or azw, or azw3 (a variation of AZW).

**Note:**  
 Now most of mobi files are drm free, becasue Amazon transform drm protected files to kfx, azw or azw3.

Compared with PRC, Mobi files have much richer metadata, such as author, title, cover image, etc. It's more compatible with reading devices, not only Android, but also Mac, Windows, iPad, with reading apps. So to sum up:

#### Why we convert prc to mobi?

\--PRC files are downloaded with Android, not compatible with other eReaders. When we want to read those downloaded files from android to iPad, Kobo, we need to convert prc to mobi.

\--After converted from prc to mobi, we can enjoy one book between multiple devices.

\--When removing drm from prc books, we have to convert prc to mobi.

\--PRC books provide us more metadata, images, writter information, etc.
