**What's VOB files?**

A VOB file is normally known as a DVD Video Object file, and is one of the most common formats for storing video and audio streams on DVD discs. VOB files are a container, which consist of 3 things. They consist of video, audio and subtitle streams.

**What's AVI videos?**

AVI stands for Audio Video Interleave. AVI files are a common format for compressed video clips. Its advantage is a simple architecture, due to which AVI runs on a number of different systems like Windows, Mac, Linux, Unix and files downloaded from the internet often use this format.
